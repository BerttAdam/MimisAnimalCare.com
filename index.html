About Mimi’s Animal Care

Hi there, I’m Miriam — but my furry, feathered, and scaly friends just call me Mimi! 🐾💜 For over 10 years, I’ve been caring for animals of all kinds — from bouncy puppies and cuddly cats to pets with special medical needs. Animals have always been my passion, and I treat every single one like part of my own family.

Whether it’s a quick potty break, a long walk, a cozy overnight stay, or an epic 3-hour adventure, I’m here to make tails wag, whiskers twitch, and feathers flutter. Every pet deserves love, patience, and plenty of playtime — and I make sure they get exactly that.

Got a special request? Need help with something not listed? Just ask! I happily customize care to fit your pet’s personality and needs. I serve Genesee, Lapeer, and Saginaw — and I’ll travel farther with a little notice.
